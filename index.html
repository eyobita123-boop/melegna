Our Story

About Melegna

መለኛ means "resourceful" in Amharic — and that's exactly what we are. We believe great cooking comes from wisdom passed down through generations, combined with a pinch of creativity. Our platform shares easy, authentic Ethiopian recipes, kitchen hacks, and sells the finest spices straight from local producers. Whether you're making Shiro or a modern fusion dish, Melegna is your kitchen companion.

The Visionary
👩🏿‍🍳

Selam Alemayehu

"Cooking is memory. Every spice tells a story of the land, the hands that harvested it, and the table it graces."

A fifth-generation Ethiopian cook and trained food scientist, Selam founded Melegna to preserve the soul of Ethiopian cuisine while making it accessible to the world. Her work has been featured in Eater, Bon Appétit, and The Guardian. She continues to share her passion through videos, workshops, and her signature spice blends.
